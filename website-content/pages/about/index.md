# About Pioneer Missions Africa

## Mission and Purpose
Pioneer Missions Africa is a ministry focused on "proclaiming the Everlasting Gospel". We believe in "the one true God (Real God) had a Son (Real Son)" and aim to spread knowledge about God and support groups across South Africa and Africa.

## Key Organizational Characteristics
- **Structure**: Not an organization, conference, or church, but a "MINISTRY"
- **Support**: We support individuals, groups, and home churches
- **Accountability**: We emphasize accountability to Christ, not to the ministry
- **Departments**: 
  - Media Department
  - Publishing Department  
  - Health Department (promoting "biblical model of Christian Health")

## Financial Approach
- We do not require tithe payments
- We accept donations when "God impresses you to support the ministry"
- We recognize the financial needs of different groups

## Core Theological Stance
- Focus on "true Knowledge of God will lead to eternal life"
- Committed to spreading what we consider the "Loud Cry message of Revelation 18"

## Our Commitment
The ministry emphasizes spiritual growth, biblical understanding, and supporting Christian communities across Africa.
